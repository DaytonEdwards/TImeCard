--Spring Season To Do List--

Games

Card games and chest board games are amazing during the spring outside, yes it may be hard to play uno with the wind and such but if you beat that, nothing will beat the joy you will have playing outside.

Feed Animals

What most people consider the most calming and cute thing to do while being at a park is feeding some wild life like a squirl, deer, duck, and more... but watch out some people have made it illegal to do that!

Park

Go to the park to relax and enjoy nature while you watch the animals cross your path and you hear the noises of the city or maybe not if youre in a more remote location.

Picking fruits

Most people go out and pick some apples, berrys, oranges, and more while enjoying the spring time, its the best even so that people pick other things like pumpkins in the fall and even pick other things in the winter.


Rugby 7's

Playing Rugby 7's is the best during the breezy weather, allowing you to enjoy the veiw while running full speed into the tri line and make some points.


Picnic

Going out with someone and taking them to sit and enjoy the breeze and some nice food is always the best move for a mid day. SOme animals might come and join too!


Camping

Go out and go camping with some friends or family and see how long you can go without begging for some internet. Its a blast to just enjoy each others comapny though so enjoy it.


Frizby

Go out if you dont want contact like rugby and throw that frizby out in the wind towards your partner and see how many times you can catch it.
